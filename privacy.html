Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

At WeatherMate, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our web application.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Location Data: We may collect location data to provide accurate weather information for your specified city. This data is not stored and is only used for the duration of your session.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about how you interact with our application, such as pages visited and features used, to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our weather services.
  • To improve our application based on user feedback and usage patterns.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates or changes to our services (if you choose to provide contact information).

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration , disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third-party services. However, we may use third-party APIs to provide weather data. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide location data, but this may limit the functionality of our application. You can also clear your browsing data to remove any stored information related to your use of our application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the provided contact options in the application.
